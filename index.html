Short Bio

I'm a PhD student in Machine Learning within the Hubert Curien Laboratory (Jean Monnet University, Saint-Étienne) under the supervision of Antoine Gourru, Charlotte Laclau and Christophe Gravier.

My research is mainly oriented towards Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing, with a particular focus on the study and mitigation of bias in Large Language Models. More recently, I also investigated the alignment of those models with human values. My PhD is funded by the French National Research Agency (ANR) in the context of the DIKE project.

I am looking for a postdoctoral position in the domains of Machine Learning fairness, reliable and responsible NLP, interpretable NLP.
